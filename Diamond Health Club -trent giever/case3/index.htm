Welcome

Home Page

At Diamond Health Club, you can stay healthy year-round and have fun doing it! We offer something for everyone. Our state-of-the-art facilities can challenge the most seasoned athlete, while remaining friendly to our first-time users. Be sure to check out our great classes for everyone from Childrens and < a href = "class.htm#Teen classes"> Teen to adult and Senior . No matter who you are, DHC offers a class for you.

DHC also provides several different membership options. You can register as an individual or a Family. We also provide special couples plans. Planning to visit Seattle a few days, weeks, or a month? Our great Tempiary are tailored to meet the needs of any visitor. Temporary memberships also make great Christmas gifts.
